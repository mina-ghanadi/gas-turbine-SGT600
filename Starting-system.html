MBJ starting system

MBJ Starter motor system

Purpose of the system The electrical starting system accelerates the gas generator to purging/ignition speed. After ignition, the gas generator is accelerated to self-sustaining speed. During shut down, that is after the combustor is shut-off, the system keeps the gas generator running at turning speed for cooling purposes
